How to Run an Executive QBR That Drives Expansion

2025-08-15 · by Lisa Carrandi

Great QBRs feel like business reviews—not status updates. Tie product usage to business outcomes and preview a joint 90-day roadmap.

  • Outcomes recap → ROI, risk reduction, or growth metrics
  • What’s working / what’s blocked
  • Executive asks and decisions
  • 90-day co-authored plan with expansion hypotheses
